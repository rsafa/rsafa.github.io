I hold a PhD in Computer Engineering (2022) and an MSc in Information Technology (2014). My research focuses on leveraging text mining and machine learning to predict the psychological states of individuals and communities from social data. I am passionate about exploring how data-driven insights can drive advancements in mental health and social understanding.


If you share similar research interests or are curious about this dynamic field, feel free to connect! you're a researcher looking for collaboration or a student interested in exploring new avenues in data science and psychology, I'm always open to discussing research opportunities and innovative projects.
